Scrambled Eggs with ham

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs stirred or beaten together typically with salt, oil, butter, milk and sometimes other ingredients, and heated so that they form into curds.

Scrambled eggs is a tasty dish and it is also very quick to do.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 knob of butter
  • 1/2 cup of chopped ham
  • Salt
  • Pepper
