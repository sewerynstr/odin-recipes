Scrambled Eggs with ham

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs stirred or beaten together typically with salt, oil, butter, milk and sometimes other ingredients, and heated so that they form into curds.

Scrambled eggs is a tasty dish and it is also very quick to do.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 knob of butter
  • 1/2 cup of chopped ham
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl. Add a small bit of salt and black pepper and continue whisking.
  2. Heat a non-stick frying pan on medium-high heat. Add a knob of butter onto the pan and wait till it melts.
  3. Add the chopped ham onto the frying pan until heated.
  4. Pour the egg mixture onto the frying pan. Cook and stir until the eggs are set but not dry, 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Transfer the eggs to a plate.
